Our story

Crafting memories since day one

Mark Event Decorating began with a simple belief: that the spaces we celebrate in should feel as meaningful as the moments themselves. What started as a love for flowers and beautiful tables has grown into a full-service styling studio trusted across Australia.

Today, our team brings together florists, stylists, and planners who share one goal: to make your event feel effortless and unforgettable. We treat every celebration as our own, pouring craft and care into every last detail.
