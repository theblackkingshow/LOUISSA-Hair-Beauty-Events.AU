About the studio

A complete styling house for occasions that matter.

We bring event decoration and salon beauty together so hosts, guests, and bridal parties can move from concept to celebration with one polished team.

Our approach

Luxury is in the coordination.

Our work is built around atmosphere: florals that suit the room, tables that feel generous, lighting that softens the space, and beauty services that complete the experience.

The studio serves weddings, birthdays, corporate events, baby showers, graduations, private celebrations, and salon clients who want reliable service with a premium finish.
