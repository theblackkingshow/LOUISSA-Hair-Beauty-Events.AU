FAQs

Helpful details before you enquire.

Do you travel?

Yes. We service Sydney and selected Australian locations, with travel quoted based on distance, scope, and setup requirements.

Can you style both event and beauty?

Yes. We can coordinate decoration, bridal or guest makeup, hair styling, and salon appointments through one team.

How early should we book?

For weddings and larger events, 3 to 9 months is ideal. Smaller celebrations can often be booked with shorter notice.

Are packages customizable?

Absolutely. Packages are starting points, and every quote can be tailored around your venue, guest count, and preferred finish.
